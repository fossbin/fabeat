With the ongoing COVID-19 issue, strengthening our immunity system is the only way to keep the virus at bay and be confident enough to overcome the viral infection even if you are affected by it. Here are our top five recommendations that are really easy to prepare and yummy to drink when it comes to liquids you can consume to boost your immunity. 

Spicy lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • Lemon juice of one medium sized lemon
  • Few gratings of ginger
  • 2-3  chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Few mint leaves
  • 1-2 tbsp. organic honey, to taste

Method:

Add all the ingredients except honey to water and bring to a boil. Strain the mixture and add honey and consume warm. 

Spiced tea

Ingredients:

  • Peppercorns – 2-3
  • Cloves – 2-3
  • Ginger grated – 1 tbsp
  • Cinnamon – 1stick
  • Crushed cardamom – 1-2
  • Star anise – 1
  • Few tulsi leaves
  • Honey to taste

Method:

Boil all the ingredients together for 25-30 minutes without honey. Strain and consume lukewarm with honey. 

Spicy turmeric-milk drink

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp freshly grated turmeric
  • ½ tsp grated ginger
  • 1.5-2 cups of soy/almond milk
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  •  1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 tbsp honey

Method:

Lightly heat all the ingredients except honey for 8-10 minutes without bringing to boil. Strain and serve warm adding the required amount of honey. 

Green smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of baby spinach
  • ½ cup of cucumber
  • 1 cup of curds
  •  ½ tsp lemon juice
  • 1-2 cups ice cubes
  • Few mint leaves
  • Salt to taste

Method:

Blend all the ingredients together until smooth and consume immediately. 

Turmeric-Ginger chai

Ingredients:

  • Water- 3 cups
  • Fresh turmeric grated 1-2 tsp
  • Grated ginger- 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice 1 tbsp
  • Honey to taste

Method:

Boil turmeric and ginger in water for 12-15 minutes, strain and add lemon juice. Serve warm with honey.
